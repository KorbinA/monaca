How to Redeem Discounts

  1. 1

    Open the discount you want to redeem in the app.

  2. 2

    When you’re ready to pay, tap while an employee is present.

  3. 3

    Show the redeemed screen to the employee and pay the business directly as usual.

Frequently Asked Questions

?What deals are national?
Our national discount network is made up of deals we get for new fundraisers all across the country and it grows with every new fundraiser we host.

When you travel to a new town and launch the app the app will search for discounts in that new city. So, say you travel to Hawaii and launch the app. It will find all discounts for any participating fundraiser groups in Hawaii. In that way, it’s like having hundreds of fundraiser cards combined into one.
?When does my card expire?
Your card will expire one year from the day you activated your account with your unlock code.
?What do I do if an employee doesn't honor my discount?
We can guarantee our discounts are real and legitimate because we never steal deals from other cards and we work with all business merchants ourselves. When a business owner agrees to sponsor a fundraiser card we send them training materials and resources to help them train their employees to recognize the cards and app.

Occasionally, a new or untrained employee will not recognize the card or know how to redeem the discount. If this happens click the button at the bottom of the discount screen to report that this happened. We will reach out to our contact(s) at that business to sort things out. 9/10 times the owner apologizes and assures us they will train that new employee or day manager to honor the discount.

We will also quickly follow up with you with the resolution of the matter.
