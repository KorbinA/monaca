How do I redeem my discount


Bring your discount to the store and press the "Use Now" button in the presence of a business employee. Pay them as usual.



The "Use Now" button is not working


Discounts are designed to only be validated while inside the business that offered it. Make sure your location services are turned on.



The business did not honor the discount


If all terms and details were complied with please email us the date, time, and location you did not receive your discount. We will follow up for you.
